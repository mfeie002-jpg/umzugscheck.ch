## 🚨 CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS

1. **Event Tracking Must Work**
   - Verify GA4 events fire on form submission
   - Without it, you're flying blind
   - Fix it BEFORE launching

2. **Negative Keywords Are Gold**
   - Add job/employment terms DAY 1
   - Monitor search terms daily first 2 weeks
   - Aggressive negatives = 30–40% cost reduction

3. **Land on Right Page**
   - High-intent (offerte, preis) → /champion-quick-flow
   - Cold/awareness (zügeln, umzug+reinigung) → /champion-trust-first
   - Wrong landing page = 50% conversion drop

4. **Offline Quality Data**
   - Don't optimize for form submit (vanity metric)
   - Optimize for bookings (real conversion)
   - Import booking data weekly → Google Ads learns

5. **Mobile Experience is Critical**
   - Sticky CTA fires on all CTAs
   - Test form on mobile first
   - Mobile often has BETTER CPL than desktop

## ⚡ COMMON MISTAKES (Don't Do These)

❌ **Multiple Landing Pages Per Keyword**
- Use SAME landing page per cluster
- Splits data = bad optimization
- Keep it simple

❌ **Testing Too Many Variables**
- Change headline AND subheadline AND button color = chaos
- Change ONE variable, wait 2 weeks, analyze
- Then change next variable

❌ **Ignoring Offline Data**
- Form submit ≠ real lead
- Some leads are junk, some convert to bookings
- Import booking data weekly

❌ **Bidding Too High Too Early**
- Start at CHF 2–3/click
- Let data inform your bids
- Don't bid based on "gut feel"

❌ **Not Adding Negatives**
- Job terms, DIY, competitor products
- Kill low-intent traffic immediately
- Saves 20–30% of budget
