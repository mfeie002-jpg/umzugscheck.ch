Usage in components:
```tsx

// Track form step completion
trackStepCompleted(1, 'Standort', 'quick_calculator');

// Track lead submission
trackLeadSubmitted({
  calculatorType: 'quick',
  moveDate: '2025-03-15',
  fromCity: 'Zürich',
  toCity: 'Bern',
  priceRange: '1200-1800',
  services: ['Umzug', 'Reinigung']
});

// Track company bookmark
trackCompanyBookmarked('company-id', 'Muster Umzüge AG', true);
```
